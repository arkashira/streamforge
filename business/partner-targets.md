## Partner Integration Roadmap for Streamforge

### 1. **AWS Timestream**
   - **Rationale**: AWS Timestream is a fully managed time-series database service that can complement Streamforge's data ingestion and processing capabilities.
   - **Free-tier limits**: 1 million writes and 1 million reads per month.
   - **Integration effort**: Medium
   - **Value-add**: Enables users to efficiently store and query time-series data, enhancing data analysis capabilities for developers and data scientists.

### 2. **InfluxDB**
   - **Rationale**: InfluxDB is a popular time-series database that can be integrated for enhanced data storage and querying capabilities.
   - **Free-tier limits**: Up to 1 million writes per month and 1 GB of storage.
   - **Integration effort**: Medium
   - **Value-add**: Provides advanced querying and visualization features, solving the job of managing large volumes of time-series data effectively.

### 3. **Grafana**
   - **Rationale**: Grafana is a leading open-source visualization tool that can enhance Streamforge's data visualization capabilities.
   - **Free-tier limits**: Up to 10,000 series and 5 dashboards.
   - **Integration effort**: Small
   - **Value-add**: Allows users to create interactive visualizations, improving the user experience for data scientists and developers.

### 4. **Datadog**
   - **Rationale**: Datadog provides monitoring and analytics for cloud applications, which can be integrated for enhanced observability of time-series data.
   - **Free-tier limits**: 5 hosts and 1-day retention.
   - **Integration effort**: Medium
   - **Value-add**: Helps users monitor application performance and troubleshoot issues, addressing the need for real-time insights.

### 5. **Prometheus**
   - **Rationale**: Prometheus is an open-source monitoring system that can be integrated to collect and store metrics as time-series data.
   - **Free-tier limits**: Open-source, no specific limits.
   - **Integration effort**: Medium
   - **Value-add**: Provides powerful querying capabilities, solving the job of monitoring and alerting on time-series data.

### 6. **Google Cloud BigQuery**
   - **Rationale**: BigQuery is a serverless data warehouse that can be leveraged for large-scale data analysis and integration with Streamforge.
   - **Free-tier limits**: 1 TB of queries per month and 10 GB of storage.
   - **Integration effort**: Large
   - **Value-add**: Enables users to perform complex queries on large datasets, enhancing analytical capabilities for data scientists.

### 7. **Zapier**
   - **Rationale**: Zapier allows integration with thousands of apps, enabling users to automate workflows with time-series data.
   - **Free-tier limits**: 5 Zaps and 100 tasks per month.
   - **Integration effort**: Small
   - **Value-add**: Simplifies the integration of Streamforge with other tools, solving the job of automating data workflows.

### 8. **Segment**
   - **Rationale**: Segment is a customer data platform that can help in collecting and routing time-series data from various sources.
   - **Free-tier limits**: 1,000 monthly tracked users.
   - **Integration effort**: Medium
   - **Value-add**: Streamlines data collection processes, addressing the need for efficient data ingestion from multiple sources.

### Conclusion
Integrating with these partners will enhance Streamforge's capabilities in time-series data management and analysis, providing significant value to users while also exploring potential affiliate and revenue-sharing opportunities.
