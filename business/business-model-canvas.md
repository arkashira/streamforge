# Business Model Canvas for Streamforge

## Customer Segments
- Data scientists and analysts in tech companies.
- Developers building applications that require time-series data management.
- Enterprises in industries such as finance, IoT, and healthcare that rely on time-series data.
- Academic institutions conducting research involving time-series analysis.

## Value Propositions
- Efficient data ingestion and processing tailored for time-series data, reducing time-to-insight.
- Advanced visualization tools that simplify complex time-series data interpretation.
- Scalability to handle large volumes of data, accommodating growing datasets.
- User-friendly interface that minimizes the learning curve for new users.

## Channels
- Direct sales through the Streamforge website.
- Partnerships with cloud service providers (e.g., AWS, Google Cloud) for bundled offerings.
- Online marketing and SEO to attract organic traffic.
- Webinars and workshops to demonstrate product capabilities and use cases.

## Customer Relationships
- Dedicated customer support via chat and email for real-time assistance.
- Community forums for user engagement and knowledge sharing.
- Regular updates and newsletters to keep customers informed about new features.
- Personalized onboarding sessions for enterprise clients.

## Revenue Streams
- Subscription model: 
  - Basic Plan: $29/month (approx. 1,000 THB)
  - Pro Plan: $99/month (approx. 3,500 THB)
  - Enterprise Plan: Custom pricing based on usage.
- One-time setup fee for enterprise clients: $5,000 (approx. 175,000 THB).
- Additional revenue from consulting services for custom integrations.

## Key Resources
- Development team skilled in data engineering and visualization.
- Cloud infrastructure for hosting and data processing.
- Intellectual property related to time-series data algorithms.
- Marketing and sales team for customer acquisition and retention.

## Key Activities
- Continuous development and enhancement of the platform's features.
- Marketing campaigns to raise awareness and attract new customers.
- Customer support and training to ensure user satisfaction.
- Data security and compliance management to protect user data.

## Key Partners
- Cloud service providers for hosting and infrastructure.
- Data analytics and visualization tool providers for integration.
- Academic institutions for research collaboration and validation.
- Industry associations for networking and market insights.

## Cost Structure
- Salaries for development, marketing, and support teams.
- Cloud hosting and infrastructure costs: estimated $2,000/month (approx. 70,000 THB).
- Marketing expenses: estimated $1,500/month (approx. 52,500 THB).
- Research and development costs for ongoing product improvements.
