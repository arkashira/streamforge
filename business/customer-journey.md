```markdown
# Customer Journey Map for Streamforge

## 1. Aware
- **Trigger Event**: Data scientists and developers encounter challenges with existing time-series data management tools, leading to inefficiencies in their workflows.
- **Friction Points**: Lack of awareness about Streamforge as a solution; existing tools are deeply ingrained in their processes.
- **User Emotions**: Frustration with current tools; curiosity about potential alternatives.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Educational content (webinars, blogs) showcasing the unique features of Streamforge; targeted ads highlighting pain points and solutions.
- **Metric**: Number of website visits and engagement with educational content.

## 2. Consider
- **Trigger Event**: Users actively search for time-series data management solutions after experiencing limitations with current tools.
- **Friction Points**: Overwhelmed by the number of options; skepticism about the effectiveness of new tools.
- **User Emotions**: Hopeful for a better solution; cautious and analytical.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Free trials or demos; case studies demonstrating successful implementations; comparison charts against competitors.
- **Metric**: Number of demo requests and trial sign-ups.

## 3. Try
- **Trigger Event**: Users sign up for a free trial of Streamforge to evaluate its capabilities.
- **Friction Points**: Initial setup complexity; learning curve associated with new features.
- **User Emotions**: Excitement mixed with apprehension; eagerness to see results.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Onboarding support (tutorials, customer support); user-friendly interface; quick wins during the trial period.
- **Metric**: Trial conversion rate and user engagement metrics during the trial.

## 4. Adopt
- **Trigger Event**: Users successfully integrate Streamforge into their workflows and see tangible benefits.
- **Friction Points**: Potential resistance from team members accustomed to previous tools; concerns about long-term commitment.
- **User Emotions**: Satisfaction from improved efficiency; relief from overcoming initial challenges.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Regular check-ins from customer success teams; showcasing advanced features that enhance productivity.
- **Metric**: User retention rate and feedback scores on satisfaction.

## 5. Expand
- **Trigger Event**: Users recognize the value of Streamforge and consider expanding its use within their organization.
- **Friction Points**: Budget constraints; uncertainty about scaling the solution across teams.
- **User Emotions**: Confidence in the product; excitement about potential growth.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Tailored solutions for different teams; incentives for expanding usage (discounts, additional features).
- **Metric**: Number of new users added and upsell conversion rates.
```
